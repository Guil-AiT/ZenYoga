About Zen

Welcome to Zen Yoga Studio
a yoga experience evolved from traditional practices in a stunning space that resonates with a perfect hum. More than just the physical practice of yoga, leave the outside world behind and immerse yourself in good vibes.

What Kind Of Yoga

While we don’t prescribe to a specific style of yoga. We base our teachings on the principles of breath-pranayama, movement-asana and awareness-meditation. These principles are the foundation of our classes in which we welcome beginners through to the most experienced yogi. Our classes are ‘warm’ and conducted in a room typically heated to 24 degrees.
Our teachers are always mindfully present for their students with the intention to create a space where you will always feel comfortable.

Yoga And Personal Space

We’re pretty sure yoga is meant to be practiced by humans and not tiny fish… so we won’t cram you in like a tin of sardines. At Humming Puppy all our classes are limited to only 20 places with an even and luxurious amount of room between each mat, so you can focus on your practice and not on another student’s behind!

Wondering Where To Start?

You can start your journey simply by signing up and registering on this site.
After you’ve purchased your classes you’re ready to book a specific class and select your mat. That’s right, we know students have their favourite spot - be it up the front, down the back or cosy in the corner - so you’re able to reserve the specific mat you want so you can simply turn up knowing you’ll be exactly where you desire!
