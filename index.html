About Our Classes

Awareness

A super chilled class.
Postures are typically seated or laying down.
These are slow paced classes that allow space and time to cultivate awareness and reflection.

Empower

Perfect place for beginners to start their journey or to review the foundations of your practice.
Classes will focus on guiding you through key asana (postures), and pranayama (breath techniques).

Dynamic

Flow and strength content designed to challenge your current limitations physically and mentally. Classes tend to focus on building into more challenging asana. Building strength and body awareness.
